# CLAUDE.md

This file provides guidance to Claude Code (claude.ai/code) when working with strategic planning and goal setting.

## Strategic Planning Management

This directory contains the strategic planning framework for Script Ohio 2.0, including vision statements, strategic objectives, goal setting, and long-term planning initiatives. This provides the foundation for all project planning and execution activities.

### Strategic Planning Components

- **Vision and Mission**: Long-term vision and mission statements
- **Strategic Objectives**: High-level objectives that support the vision
- **Goal Setting**: Specific, measurable goals to achieve strategic objectives
- **Strategic Initiatives**: Major programs and projects to achieve goals
- **Performance Metrics**: Measures to track strategic progress and success

### Strategic Planning Hierarchy

1. **Vision Statement**: Aspirational future state (3-5+ years)
2. **Mission Statement**: Purpose and core business definition
3. **Strategic Objectives**: Broad areas of focus (1-3 years)
4. **Strategic Goals**: Specific, measurable outcomes (6-18 months)
5. **Initiatives**: Programs and projects to achieve goals (3-12 months)
6. **Actions**: Specific tasks and activities (1-3 months)

## Strategic Framework

### Vision Statement
- **Time Horizon**: 3-5+ year outlook
- **Aspirational Nature**: Bold, inspiring, and challenging
- **Clear Direction**: Provides clear direction for all planning
- **Stakeholder Focus**: Addresses needs of all key stakeholders
- **Memorable**: Easy to remember and communicate

### Mission Statement
- **Purpose Definition**: Clear statement of organizational purpose
- **Core Business**: What the organization does and for whom
- **Value Proposition**: Unique value delivered to stakeholders
- **Guiding Principles**: Core values and guiding principles
- **Differentiation**: What makes the organization unique

### Strategic Objectives
- **Broad Areas**: 4-6 broad strategic focus areas
- **Long-term Focus**: 1-3 year time horizon
- **Measurable**: Clear criteria for measuring success
- **Aligned**: Aligned with vision and mission
- **Actionable**: Can be translated into specific goals and initiatives

### Strategic Goals
- **SMART Criteria**: Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound
- **Quantitative**: Clear numerical targets and metrics
- **Time-bound**: Specific deadlines and timeframes
- **Accountable**: Clear ownership and responsibility
- **Challenging**: Stretch goals that drive improvement

## Strategic Planning Process

### Strategic Analysis
1. **Environmental Scan**: Analysis of external environment and trends
2. **Internal Assessment**: Evaluation of internal capabilities and resources
3. **Stakeholder Analysis**: Understanding stakeholder needs and expectations
4. **Competitive Analysis**: Analysis of competitive landscape and positioning
5. **SWOT Analysis**: Comprehensive analysis of strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, and threats

### Strategy Formulation
1. **Vision Setting**: Define long-term vision and aspirations
2. **Mission Definition**: Clarify organizational purpose and value proposition
3. **Objective Setting**: Establish strategic objectives and focus areas
4. **Goal Development**: Create specific, measurable strategic goals
5. **Initiative Planning**: Define major programs and initiatives

### Strategy Implementation
1. **Action Planning**: Develop detailed action plans and timelines
2. **Resource Allocation**: Allocate resources to support strategic priorities
3. **Organization Alignment**: Align structure and processes with strategy
4. **Performance Management**: Establish performance metrics and monitoring
5. **Communication Plan**: Communicate strategy throughout organization

### Strategy Review
1. **Progress Monitoring**: Track progress against strategic goals
2. **Performance Assessment**: Evaluate strategic performance and outcomes
3. **Environmental Scanning**: Monitor changes in external environment
4. **Strategy Adjustment**: Adjust strategy based on performance and changes
5. **Learning and Improvement**: Capture lessons learned and improve process

### Initiative Plans
- **Initiative Charters**: Charter documents for major strategic initiatives

- **Implementation Plans**: Detailed implementation plans and timelines

- **Risk Management**: Initiative-specific risk assessment and mitigation

## Strategic Performance Management

### Performance Metrics
- **Leading Indicators**: Early indicators of strategic progress
- **Lagging Indicators**: Results and outcomes of strategic execution
- **Financial Metrics**: Financial performance and return on investment
- **Operational Metrics**: Operational efficiency and effectiveness
- **Customer Metrics**: Customer satisfaction and value delivery

### Performance Monitoring
- **Dashboard Reporting**: Visual performance dashboards and scorecards
- **Regular Reviews**: Monthly and quarterly performance reviews
- **Trend Analysis**: Analysis of performance trends and patterns
- **Variance Analysis**: Analysis of actual vs. planned performance
- **Root Cause Analysis**: Analysis of performance issues and drivers

### Performance Improvement
- **Performance Gaps**: Identification of performance gaps and issues
- **Improvement Initiatives**: Programs to improve strategic performance
- **Best Practice Sharing**: Sharing of best practices and lessons learned
- **Capability Building**: Building organizational capabilities to support strategy
- **Process Optimization**: Improving processes to better support strategic execution

## Strategic Review Cadence

### Annual Strategic Review
- **Comprehensive Assessment**: Full review of strategic plan and performance
- **Environmental Update**: Update of environmental analysis and assumptions
- **Strategy Refresh**: Refresh and update strategic objectives and goals
- **Resource Planning**: Annual resource planning and allocation
- **Leadership Alignment**: Leadership team alignment on strategic direction

### Quarterly Strategic Review
- **Progress Assessment**: Review of progress against strategic goals
- **Performance Dashboard**: Review of strategic performance metrics
- **Initiative Status**: Status review of major strategic initiatives
- **Risk Monitoring**: Review of strategic risks and mitigation activities
- **Adjustment Planning**: Planning for adjustments and improvements

### Monthly Strategic Check-in
- **Metric Review**: Review of key strategic performance metrics
- **Initiative Updates**: Status updates on strategic initiatives
- **Issue Resolution**: Address strategic issues and roadblocks
- **Resource Coordination**: Coordinate resources for strategic priorities
- **Communication Updates**: Share strategic updates with organization
